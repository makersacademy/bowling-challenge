Rules:

  • There are 10 frames in each game.
  • In each frame you get two rolls with the aim of knocking down as many of the 10 pins as you can.
  • After each frame the pins get reset.
  • You score a strike if you knock all 10 pins down in roll, and a spare if you knock all 10 pins down in two rolls.
  • You will score bonus points for achieving a strike or spare.
  • If you score a strike or spare in frame 10 you will be given a bonus roll.
