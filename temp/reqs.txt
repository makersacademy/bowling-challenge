- Game = includes 10 frames
- Frame = includes 2 rolls
- Score = is the total points (pins knocked down) in 1 frame

Other - good to know:
- roll = user inputs how many pins knocked down
- pins = there are 10 pins within a Frame

Scoring:
- Strikes = 10 points + bonus form next frame (2 rolls)

Spares:
- A "spare" is awarded when no pins are left standing after the second ball of a frame
- the points from first roll of next frame is called a spare

10th frame:
- 3 balls in the frame e.g 10, 10, 10 in the 10th frame gives 30 points (10 points for the regular first strike and 20 points for the bonus).
1, 9, 10 in the 10th frame gives 20 points (10 points for the regular spare and 10 points for the bonus)

=====

Rules for Scoring:
- Score = is the total points (pins knocked down) in 1 frame
- Gutter Game = 0 points (20 zero scores, no pins hit)
- Perfect Game = A Perfect Game is when the player rolls 12 strikes (10 regular strikes and 2 strikes for the bonus in the 10th frame). The Perfect Game scores 300 points.
